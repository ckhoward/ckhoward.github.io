about

Hi. I’m an aspiring data scientist living in Tucson, Arizona.

I created this site as a means to learn. I find it to be a useful way to articulate my thoughts, and I’ve learned that the social element helps with retention. If I talk to somebody about something I just read, it becomes more ingrained in memory. While blogging can be audienceless and is written rather than spoken, I’m hoping to acheive the same social effect.

Additionally, I will use the projects page to hold myself accountable for starting and finishing more projects. Maybe somebody will see something they like. Maybe I will get constructive criticism. Regardless, I’m hoping to inspire readers at least a little bit, and improve my communication skills in the process.

I like to write about politics, mind, terrorism, cyber-security, disinformation, and culture.

Types of Analyses: statistical, geospatial, natural language, and social network

Tools: Python, R, JavaScript (d3.js), ArcGIS
